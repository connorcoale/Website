About Me

I am a fourth year student at Dartmouth College studying Engineering Sciences modified with Computer Science. I will be completing my B.A. in Spring 2022, and will complete my Bachelors of Engineering in Computer Engineering in March 2023.

In the past I've interned as a Manufacturing Engineering Intern for Kadant Black Clawson, as an Engineering Intern at Medtronic, and as a Software Intern at Tufts University's Human-Robot Interaction lab.

As someone who came into college expecting to follow a path in the Mechanical Engineering route, I have a strong background in real-world design and manufacturing which I am able to apply to any projects that align more closely with Computer Engineering. I think that this gives me a unique perspective as a developer. As I took more electrical and computer engineering courses thanks to the breadth-focused curriculum at Dartmouth's Thayer School of Engineering, I realized that the hardware/software field was what picqued my interest as an engineer the most. Even if I wish that I might have realized this a bit earlier, I am still very appreciative of the work and school experiences I've been fortunate to have in the Mechanical field.

My experience as a Computer Engineer has afforded me particular strength in the following focuses:

  • Embedded Systems
  • FPGA programming
  • Computer Networking
I am continuing to gain more experience as I more forward towards graduation, and I can't wait to find out where this field will take me!
